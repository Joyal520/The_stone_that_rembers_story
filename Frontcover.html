The Legend

Vihaan’s first assignment should have been simple: deliver a sealed document across the Pinnacle Desert before morning. No delays. No questions. No mistakes.

But deserts remember.

When an accident leaves a mysterious stone disturbed, time begins to fracture. Roads repeat. Shadows whisper. Night refuses to move forward. Trapped in a looping stretch of sand, Vihaan discovers that the desert is not empty—it is guarded by ancient forces, bound by stones meant to keep something far worse asleep.

As fear twists reality and repetition erodes his memory, Vihaan must choose between escape and responsibility. To restore balance, he must face what the desert demands—and what it will take from him in return.

This is not a story about survival alone.

It is about what happens after balance is restored…
and what the desert keeps.
